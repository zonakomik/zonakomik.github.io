1. story telling about the youth pledge


Jawaban:

The Youth Pledge is a major milestone in the history of the Indonesian Independence movement. October 28 is celebrated as Youth Pledge Day, this is because the Youth Pledge was held on October 27-28, 1928 in Batavia (Jakarta). The Youth Pledge was a decision of the Second Youth Congress and this pledge was considered as a spirit to affirm the confiscation of the founding of the Indonesian state. The Youth Pledge itself was formulated and written by Mr. Prof. Mohammad Yamin, S.H. on a sheet of paper.

Fill in the Youth Pledge

The second youth congress was attended by various participants, namely representatives of Indonesian students (PPPI), Jong Java, Jong Sumatranen Bond, Jong Bataks Bond, Jong Islamienten Bond, Indonesian Youth, Jong Celebes, Jong Ambon and others.

The building, which is located on Jl Keramat Raya 106 Central Jakarta, is the venue for the congress, which currently houses the Youth Pledge Museum.

In the Congress there were 3 important decisions, namely the Youth Pledge which read:

1. Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, mengaku bertumpah darah yang satu tanah air Indonesia.

2. Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, mengaku berbangsa yang satu bangsa Indonesia.

3. Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, menjunjung bahasa persatuan bahasa Indonesia.

( 1. We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, claim to be spilled by the blood of one Indonesian homeland.

2. We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, claim to be one Indonesian nation.

3. We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, uphold the unified Indonesian language.)

The Purpose of the Youth Pledge

After knowing the brief history and contents of the Youth Pledge, we must also know what the true purpose of the Youth Pledge is.

This time, Geniora news will summarize the purpose of holding the Youth Pledge:

1. Awakening the spirit and attitude of nationalism of the Indonesian youth and all Indonesian people to fight, expel and oppose the colonialists.

2. Expanding activities to finance activities in order to achieve Indonesian Independence.

3. Carry out the aspiration to gather all Indonesian youth.

4. Creating a strong and thick sense of unity and integrity of the Indonesian nation.

The Meaning of the Youth Pledge

The meaning of the Youth Pledge is of course very important to be implanted in the soul, the contents of the Youth Pledge affirm the ideals of the existence of "Indonesian Homeland", "Indonesian Nation", "Indonesian Language".

Here are some of the meanings of the Youth Pledge that has been summarized by Geniora News:

1. Teach the values of national unity.

2. A form of love for the country.

3. Encourage youth fighting spirit.

4. As an invitation to maintain the integrity of the Indonesian nation

5. Fostering pride as the Indonesian nation.

6. Emphasize pride in the Indonesian language.

4. the writer and his friends returned to their lodge ... it was dark there​


THE PARTS OF SPEECH: CONJUNCTION

Conjunction adalah kata atau kelompok kata yang berfungsi menghubungkan dua kalimat atau paragraf.

Pembahasan

Segala perkataan dalam bahasa Inggris dikelompokkan ke dalam bagian bagian berdasarkan arti mereka dan fungsinya, bagian-bagian dasar itu yang kemudian disebut sebagai Parts of Speech. Ada 8 parts of speech, yaitu: noun, pronoun, verb, adjective, adverb, preposition, conjunction, dan interjection

Pada pembahasan ini kita akan menjawab soal di atas. Complete the sentence with appropriate CONJUNCTION

"The writer and his friends returned to their lodge ... it was dark there"

Kata yang tepat untuk mengisi kalimat di atas adalah "when", menentukan konjungsi dapat dilakukan dengan cara melihat kalimat kedua yang menyambung dengannya, perhatikan pesan dari kalimat itu. Apakah kalimat itu menunjukkan sebuah waktu atau sebuah tempat

Untuk kalimat di atas, terlihat dengan jelas bahwa kalimat keduanya adalah sebuah keterangan waktu "when it was dark there". Dan kata hubung yang tepat untuk menyambungkan suatu kalimat dengan keterangan waktu adalah kata "when" yang berarti "ketika"

8. write a story about "School" dan terjemahan bahasa indonesianya


PART = DESCRIPTIVE TEXT

------------------

Jawaban :

My name is Dee. I’m thirteen years old. I study at SMPN  “Brainly Nuansa Indah.” My school is elite and very famous in my town. Many students come from different regions study in this school. ( IDENTIFICATION )

My school is green and shady. There are many variety of plants and flowers. The trees produce the fresh air around the school and it makes more comfortable to stay. My school has many rooms . There are a spacious library , a large parking shade, a headmaster’s office, school canteens, language laboratories, science laboratories, multimedia rooms, toilets, a hall and twenty rooms for seventh grade class until ninth grade class. My school’s yard is very large. It is in front of the ninth grade classes. We can play kinds of sport games in the school’s yard. My school provides the sport equipment completely because my school creates the sport clubs to train for those who are talented in the sport game.  ( DESCRIPTION )

When we enter the school,   we will see the greeting billboard which is written “Welcome to SMPN “Brainly Nuansa Indah”  in front of the school building gate. There is a security post beside the gate. There are two securities guard the post. They have a responsibility to keep our school safe. That is a story about my school. I love my school very much because of its conduciveness. ( DESCRIPTION )

TRANSLATION

Nama ku Dee.  Aku berusia tiga belas tahun. Aku bersekolah  di SMPN "Brainly Nuansa Indah." Sekolah ku  elit dan sangat terkenal di kota ku. Banyak siswa datang dari berbagai daerah untuk belajar  di sekolah ini.  

Sekolahku  hijau dan teduh. Ada beragam  tanaman dan bunga. Pohon-pohon memberikan udara segar di sekitar sekolah dan itu membuat lebih nyaman untuk menetap. Sekolah ku  memiliki banyak ruangan. Ada perpustakaan yang luas, tempat parkir yang luas, kantor kepala sekolah, kantin sekolah, laboratorium bahasa, laboratorium sains, ruang multimedia, toilet, aula, dan dua puluh ruangan untuk kelas tujuh hingga kelas sembilan. Halaman sekolah ku sangat luas. Halaman letaknya di depan kelas sembilan. Kami dapat memainkan berbagai jenis permainan olahraga di halaman sekolah. Sekolah ku  menyediakan peralatan olahraga dengan lengkap karena sekolah ku membuat  klub-klub  olahraga untuk melatih bagi mereka yang berbakat dalam permainan olahraga.

Ketika kami  memasuki sekolah, kami akan melihat papan pengumuman ucapan yang tertulis "Selamat datang di SMPN" Brainly Nuansa Indah "di depan gerbang gedung sekolah. Ada pos keamanan di samping gerbang. Ada dua satpam  yang menjaga pos. Mereka memiliki tanggung jawab untuk menjaga keamanan sekolah kami . Itu adalah cerita tentang sekolah ku. Aku sangat mencintai sekolah ku karena kondusifitasnya ( damai )

Penjelasan :

DESCRIPTIVE TEXT Genre teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik. Social function/ communicative purpose : to describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus ) Generic Structure  

1.  Identification  

Bagian paragraf ini  terletak di awal sebagai pengenalan siapa/apa yang akan dijabarkan dalam teks

2.  Description  

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas-nya bagaimana dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

3. Conclusion  

Paragraf akhir berisi kesimpulan dan pendapat dari si penulis. Bagian ini optional ( Bisa dibahas atau pun tidak )
