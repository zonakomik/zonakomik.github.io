20. apakah perbedaan part, season, cour, dan chapter dlm suatu film/komik ?​


Berikut ini penjelasan mengenai perbedaan part, season, cour, serta chapter yang biasa dijumpai pada film, buku atau komik:

Part atau bagian dalam film atau komik merujuk pada subdivisi utama yang membagi cerita menjadi bagian-bagian yang lebih kecil. Contohnya, sebuah film dengan tiga bagian yang berbeda dapat disebut sebagai "trilogi". Bagian-bagian tersebut sering kali memiliki alur cerita dan fokus yang berbeda.Season atau musim dalam konteks televisi atau anime merujuk pada serangkaian episode yang ditayangkan dalam periode waktu tertentu, biasanya dalam per sekian bulan atau bahkan satu tahun. Biasanya satu musim memiliki 12 hingga 24 episode, tergantung pada genre dan durasi episode.Cour atau kouru dalam anime adalah istilah yang digunakan untuk menggambarkan pembagian episode dalam satu musim. Istilah "cour"  awalnya digunakan menggambarkan rentang episode selama satu musim. Setiap kouru biasanya terdiri atas 10 - 14 episodeChapter adalah nama lain 'bab' yang biasa kita jmpai dalam komik atau buku. Chapter merujuk pada bagian-bagian besar cerita yang terpisah satu sama lain. Setiap chapter biasanya memiliki memiliki kisah dan alur cerita tersendiri meskipun tentu masih berhubungan dengan cerita utama.Pembahasan

Secara umum bisa dikatakan bahwa part, season, dan cour mengacu pada pembagian cerita pada media audiovisual. Adapun chapter, lebih cenderung mengacu pada pembagian cerita dalam bentuk media cetak seperti komik dan buku. Meski begitu, beberapa anime juga menggunakan istilah chapter untuk mengacu pada episode dalam serinya.

Jawaban:

Bahasa Inggris kelas X chapter 5 adalah menjawab pertanyaan yang berhubungan dengan teks Visiting Niagara Falls.

1. Where is Niagara Falls located?

Niagara Falls is located between the Canadian province of Ontario and the USA’s state of New York.

2. Mention the three waterfalls that form the Niagara Falls.

There are the Horseshoe Falls, the American Falls, and the Bridal Veil Falls

3. What can people enjoy in the Cave of the Winds?

People can enjoy get closer to the falls and go face-to-face with the pounding waters of the Falls in the Cave of the Winds.

4. Can people ride on the Maid of the Mist Boat Tour in January?

No, they can't. Because The boat operates mid-May until late October.

5. Where can people watch a film of the thundering falls with completely different background?

People watch a film of the thundering falls with completely different background in Niagara Adventure Theater.

6. Can the tourists enjoy the film in their own language?

Yes, they can. Because The theater shows hourly and free multi language headsets are made available.

7. What is kept in Niagara Science Museum?

A sanctuary for the preservation and appreciation of old science instruments and philosophical apparatus are kept in Niagara Science Museum.

8. What is shown in Niagara’s Wax Museum of History?

Life-size wax figures portraying dramatic history of Niagara Falls is shown in Niagara's wax Museum of History.

9. Where can people see the story of how electricity was made?

People can see the story of how electricity was made in Niagara’s Wax Museum of History.

10. Is it possible for people to have Rainbow Air Helicopter Tours at night?

No, it is not. Because it opens from 9 a.m. to dusk when weather permits.

1. Di mana Air Terjun Niagara berada?

Air Terjun Niagara terletak di antara provinsi Ontario di Kanada dan negara bagian New York di AS.

2. Sebutkan tiga air terjun yang membentuk Air Terjun Niagara.

Ada Air Terjun Horseshoe, Air Terjun Amerika, dan Air Terjun Kerudung Pengantin

Orang-orang dapat menikmati lebih dekat air terjun dan berhadapan langsung dengan air terjun di Gua Angin.

26. teks narrative bahasa inggris " The Lion and The Hare"


A lion was getting fed up of hunting. He called all animals and passed an order, "Everyday one of you should come by yourself as my prey". All animals started to obey this order. 

It was the hare's day. The hare was very sad. As he was going to the lion's den, he came across an old well. He looked into it. It was very deep and dangerous. He made a plan to his mind. 

The lion was very angry that no animal had come that day. The hare slowly came towards the lion. The lion roared "Why are you late?" The hare humbly replied, "Oh my way another lion chased me. I got away with much difficulty to give my life to you, Your Majesty". 

The lion was pleased with the hare. But the thought of another lion in the jungle made him angry. The lion roared "Do you know where he lives?" The hare replied "Yes, Sir. Please come with me". 

The hare took the lion to the old well. He said "Sir, that lion lives in this well". The lion peeped into the well. He mistook his reflection for another lion. He roared and there was an echo. He thought that the other lion was roaring too and jumped into the well. That was the end of the lion. 

MORAL : Wisdom can win might.

27. hare hare ya artinya​


Jawaban :

'Hare hare ya' artinya "Terang benderang"

| | | | | | | | | | | | | | | | | | | |

Pembahasan :

Singkat aja ya ;)

Jadi kalimat ini terdiri dari dua kata, yaitu 'Hare' yang berarti "Cerah", dan 'Ya' yang merupakan partikel penegas kalimat yang biasa digunakan di daerah Kansai. Jika diartikan secara keseluruhan, kalimat ini bisa diartikan sebagai "Terang benderang".

Untuk partikel 'Ya' ini memiliki fungsi lain selain sebagai penegas, antara lain yaitu :

Menyebutkan beberapa benda yang sepadan. Kalau dalam Bahasa Indonesia sama seperti kata hubung "Dan" atau bisa juga sebagai tanda koma ",". Contohnya seperti 'Neko ya inu', artinya "Kucing dan aπjing".Sebagai panggilan atau sapaan pada nama. Untuk fungsi ini jarang digunakan karena biasa hanya digunakan di daerah Kansai. Contoh penggunaannya misalkan nama saya, jadi Azuka-ya! (pengucapan dengan nada naik).

33. bagaimana cerita the hare and the the tortoise dalam bahasa inggris



The Hare and The Tortoise

     One day, the hare said to the other animals, ” I’m the fastest animal in the world!”. All of the animals were amazed, except the tortoise. The tortoise said to the hare, ”Are you sure? I can beat you. Do you want to race?”. The hare laughed and said, ”Are you crazy? I’m much faster than you!”. The tortoise said “Then, let’s race!”.

     The hare accepted the challenge and the race is ready to start. The participants are the hare and the tortoise. And all of the animals are watching them on the other side.

     The race started, and the hare ran quickly down the road. The tortoise smiled, and walked slowly after the hare. The hare looked back and laughed, ”Hahaha, i knew you're such a loser!”. The tortoise smiled again and now he said, ”Okay, we’ll see that later!”.

     After thirty minutes later, the hare felt tired. He looked back and didn’t see the tortoise. The hare said,”Huhuhuhuh.. Hmmm. The tortoise will never beat me! I think.. I’ll take a short nap.” Then the hare went to sleep.

     A hour later, the tortoise came down the road walking very slowly. He saw the hare sleeping under the tree. "That must be the arrogant Hare!" But, he didn’t stop. He continued walking.

     Finally, the hare woke up. He saw the tortoise near the finish line. He said,”Ohh, where is the tortoise?! Oh, it’s over there! I must run faster than him!”. But unfortunately, he was too late. The tortoise won the race!

    "Ho..How could you...? Alright, i'm so sorry for that, Tortoise." The tortoise accepts his apology, and finally they are best friends forever that are supposed to support each other.
