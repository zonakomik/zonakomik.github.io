-----------------

PREFACE

Personal pronoun atau kata ganti (orang) adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menggantikan nama subjek atau objek dalam kalimat. Personal pronoun dalam tata bahasa Inggris menunjukkan kedudukan (subjek/objek), person (orang pertama/kedua/ketiga), dan jumlah (tunggal/jamak). Pronoun tidak hanya menggantikan orang tapi juga benda dan binatang.

Saya = I (subjek) - Me (objek)

Kamu = You (subjek) - You (objek)

Dia (l) = He (subjek) - Him (objek)

Dia (p) = She (subjek) - Her (objek)

Dia (bukan orang) = It (subjek) - It (objek)

Kami/kita = We (subjek) - Us (objek)

Kalian = You (subjek) - You (objek)

Mereka (orang dan bukan orang) = They (subjek) - Them (objek)

-----------------

EXPLICATION

1. Subjek = JOHN = he  

Objek = NANCY = her

2. Subjek = JANE = she

HER BROTHER = him

4. Subjek = MY SISTER AND I = we

5. Subjek = MY AUNT  = she

6. Subjek = YOU AND I = us

Objek = JOHN = him

7. Subjek = THE STUDENTS = they  

Objek = THE PROBLEM = it

8. Objek = THIS ONE = it

THE BABY jika dianggap benda = it; jika dianggap orang = him (u/ laki-lai) atau her (u/ perempuan).

9. Subjek = HENDRY AND ALEX = they

Objek = A CAKE = it

MY SISTER AND ME = us

10. Subjek = MY FRIEND AND I = us

Objek = JOHN = him

Rumus yg digunakan utk membuat Pertanyaan:

* Verbal Sentence (Active)

(?) WH + DID + S + VERB 1 + O?

1. When + DID + the writer/he/she + GO + to the dolphin show?

2. Where + DID + the writer/he/she + TAKE + a seat?

3. Why + DID + the writer/he/she raise his/her hand?

4. What + DID + the instruction tell the dolphin?

* Nominal Sentence (Active)

(?) Was/were + S + O?

5. Was + the writer/he/she + happy?
