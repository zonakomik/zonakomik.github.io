33. The wife-My-of-father-my-is-Mother


Jawaban: My mother is the wife of my Father

Penjelasan:Semoga membantu : )

Jawaban:

|| ARRANGE THE JUMBLE WORDS INTO CORRECT SENTENCE ||

The wife-My-of-father-my-is-Mother

→ The wife of my father is my mother.

Arti :

→ Istri dari ayahku adalah ibuku.

Penjelasan:

|| KALIMAT NOMINAL SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE ||

Rumus :

→ S + to be (is/am/are) + non-verb

Contoh :

The son of my daughter is my grandson.The sister of my father is my aunt.The father of my mother is my grandfather.The son of my mother is my brother.The son of my brother is my nephew.

Analisis pola kalimat diatas :

The wife of my father → SubjectIs → to beMy mother → non-verb
